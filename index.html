SHIROHIGE

Shirohige, also known as Edward Newgate or Whitebeard, was one of the most formidable and respected pirates in the Ocean. He was the captain of the Whitebeard Pirates and one of the Four Emperors, the most powerful pirate captains in the world. Physically, Shirohige was a massive and imposing figure with a towering height and a muscular build. He had a distinctive appearance with a long white beard that earned him his nickname. He often wore a white captain's coat adorned with symbols representing his crew.
